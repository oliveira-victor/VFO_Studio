About VFO Studio

Created by Victor Freire Oliveira, VFO is a digital art, design and animation studio based in Maceió, AL - Brazil. The use of creativity, the constant search for inspiration, the knowledge about design and the love for arts make VFO have a reason to exist, choosing to be friendly, original, creative, simple and artistic in all of the creations. VFO Studio has preference for Linux based operating systems and free and open source softwares for most of its creations.

"Throughout the years, VFO Studio has helped many clients express their ideas, finding solution to their problems in a creative way. From illustration to be gifted to the loved ones, to logos, videos and book illustration, people look for my services with different goals.

I'm a freelancer artist and designer who likes to play with creativity and create inspiring things for myself and for people who need this kind of way of expressing ideas and feelings. You can also contact me and let me know what kind of creative service you need, or maybe discuss ideas and suggest some partnership." :)

- Victor Freire Oliveira.
