Book & magazine illustration

Il Bambulino Tessitore

Children's book written by Marina Natascia Finazzi. The chosen art style is a very simplified painting in crayon, as it's a story for toddlers, so it was meant to be simple and clean, easy to understand. The story itself was challenging as the main character is a belly button, so that's the solution I came up with.

La Caccolina Che Voleva Vedere il Mondo

Another book by Marina Natascia Finazzi, also for toddlers, having the same style used in the previous book. The challenge was accepted and I created a character that represents a booger. It was a fun project to work with that made me take the best from my creativity.
